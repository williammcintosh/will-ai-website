1-to-1 Training

Focused coaching for owners and key staff who need direct guidance. No bundling and no obligation to buy implementation.

Bespoke 1-to-1 options:

  • Review Habit Reset: challenge is inconsistent asking; outcomes are a repeatable ask habit; owner does daily asks and logs; I coach the script, timing, and follow-up.
  • Reply Confidence Coaching: challenge is avoiding replies; outcomes are calm, consistent responses; owner replies weekly; I coach tone, triage, and risk basics.
  • Profile Stewardship: challenge is outdated info; outcomes are a simple update routine; owner checks and updates monthly; I coach the checklist and quality control.
  • Local Presence Sprint: challenge is low local trust; outcomes are steady visibility habits; owner publishes small updates; I coach the cadence and quality bar.
