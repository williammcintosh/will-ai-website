Digital tools: artificial intelligence for small business

Practical AI training for everyday business admin, marketing drafts, and customer comms. This is training, not implementation.

Who it is for

  • Owners and managers who want faster admin work.
  • Team members writing emails, replies, or updates.
  • Businesses with small marketing or admin capacity.

What you learn

  • Safe, practical AI use for SMEs.
  • How to brief AI clearly and keep your tone.
  • Use cases for review replies, quotes, and follow ups.

What you leave with

  • A prompt library for common business tasks.
  • Templates for emails, replies, and follow ups.
  • A simple AI use checklist for staff.
