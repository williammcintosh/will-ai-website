Training Workshops

Hands-on workshops for teams who want shared habits. No bundling and no obligation to buy implementation.

Workshop options (eligible subjects):

  • Review Request Systems: for front desk and managers; learn when to ask and what to say; produce a simple ask script and follow-up checklist.
  • Review Reply Confidence: for owners and admins; learn tone, response triage, and risk basics; produce a reply style guide and response templates.
  • Google Business Profile Essentials: for owners and team leads; learn what matters in the profile and how to keep it accurate; produce a monthly profile checklist.
  • Local Visibility Basics: for small teams doing their own marketing; learn simple on-page and Q&A habits; produce a content prompt list and weekly cadence plan.
