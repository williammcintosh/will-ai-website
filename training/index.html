Training

Two lanes: Training builds capability in your team, implementation is a separate paid service. No bundling and no obligation to buy implementation.

Choose workshops for shared team learning or 1-to-1 coaching for owner-led change.

Typical training engagement:

  • Short discovery call to set the focus and who should attend.
  • In-person Auckland session or remote session if needed.
  • Simple playbook, scripts, and checklists to run in-house.
  • Owner-led practice and follow-up Q&A to lock in habits.
