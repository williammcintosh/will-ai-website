Privacy Policy

Review Fuel respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

What we collect

We may collect personal information you provide to us, including your name, business name, email address, phone number, location, and any details you submit through our contact forms or lead forms.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide services, manage client relationships, improve our offerings, and communicate with you about work related to your request. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing information

We only share personal information with trusted service providers where necessary to operate our business, such as form handling or messaging services. These providers are required to protect your data.

Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and protect it from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

Access and correction

You have the right to request access to your personal information and ask for corrections if it is inaccurate. Contact us to do so.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use basic analytics and cookies to understand site usage and improve performance. These do not identify you personally.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at will@reviewfuel.co.nz.
