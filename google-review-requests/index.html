Get more Google reviews the normal way

If customers are not leaving Google reviews, it is usually not because they hated the service. They just forget. We run simple post transaction follow up so your happy customers actually leave a Google review.

Not a promo blast. A polite review request.

Review Fuel works with small to medium New Zealand businesses with physical locations or local service areas. Messages are sent after a real sale or service, tied to an actual interaction.

How it works

If you are searching “how to get more Google reviews”, this is the simplest answer. We text your customers on your behalf, soon after the job, in a human tone.

1. We write a message that sounds like you

We draft a Google review request SMS that includes the business name, the customer name, and a short reference to what they bought or what you did. It is not spammy. It is one clean request with an opt out.

2. Send after the job, not weeks later

Timing is the whole game. Review request follow up works best when it is close to the real interaction. We help you pick a delay that fits your workflow, so it feels natural and not weird.

3. Keep it low volume and legit

Messages go only to existing customers who already shared their number at point of sale or through a booking or enquiry form. This is transactional follow up, not a marketing list. The goal is authentic reviews, not fake hype.
